JCSU Students Attend Conference in Washington DC


Principal investigator, Dr. Brown-Fox presented “Establishing A Thriving, Sustainable Undergraduate Polymer Research Program” at the HBCU-UP PI/PD meeting in Washington, D.C. March 13-14, 2024. Also, students, Mr. Saeed Dupree (Biology and Chemistry) and Joshua Beatty (Biology) from the College of STEM and the M-PURE program were chosen to present their research at the 2024 Emerging Researchers National (ERN) Conference in STEM in Washington, D.C., March 14-16th, 2024. Students' research projects were supported by NSF Award: #2101209.
